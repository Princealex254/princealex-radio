Our Story

Prince Alex Radio was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to be the voice of the community. We believe in the power of music and conversation to connect people, inspire change, and celebrate culture. Our journey began in Kenya, driven by a passion for creating a platform that is authentic, engaging, and truly represents the diverse voices of our listeners.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide high-quality, entertaining, and informative programming that enriches the lives of our listeners and strengthens our community.

Vision: To be the leading digital radio station in Kenya, recognized for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and community engagement.
